About Giovanni

Who I Am

Hello! I'm Giovanni Michel, a first-generation Electrical Engineer and Computer Engineer. I earned my degree in Electrical Engineering from Northwestern University and my degree in Computer Engineering and Artificial Intelligence from Florida Atlantic University. I worked at Los Alamos National Laboratory (LANL) for over 3 years as a Machine Learning researcher.

At LANL, I had the opportunity to work at the intersection of Machine Learning, Control Theory, and Robotics. My passion lies in developing intelligent autonomous systems, and I'm actively seeking opportunities to advance robotics and AI technologies that can solve real-world challenges.
